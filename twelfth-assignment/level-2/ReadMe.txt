az oonjayi ke har methodi benevisim faqat baraye datatypehaye primitive kar mikone,
farqi nadare ke az kodum yeki az methodhaye zir estefade konim:

Contains(), Equals(), Intersect(), SequenceEquals() va ....

baraye datatypehaye dige va datatypehaye user-defined,
ya bayad methode digari neveshte beshe,
ya bayad ba tavajoh be inke kodum yeki as methodhaye moqayese ke bala mesal zade shod estefade beshe dakhele methode ma,
ya hata agar == estefade beshe,
yeki az interfacehaye IEqualityComparer ya IEquatable dar un datatype implement beshe.
